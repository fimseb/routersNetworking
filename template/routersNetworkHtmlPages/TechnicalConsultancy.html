Following steps for new ISP business setup

  1. Create a private limited company.
  2. Get ISP/ VNO license from Department of Telecommunication.
  3. Get IP pool /AS Number from authorized department.
  4. Purchase hardware.
  5. Get ILL from Telco.
  6. Get Google peering from CDN/Data center.
  7. Start internet business in your area.
